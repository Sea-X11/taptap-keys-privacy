App: TapTap Keys (弹弹键)
Developer: Sea-X11
Effective Date: April 1, 2026

1. No Data Collection

TapTap Keys does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

2. No Microphone Access

Although iOS may list microphone permission, TapTap Keys does not request or use the microphone at any time.

3. In-App Purchases

The optional "Rhythm Guide Pro" purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or handle any payment information.

4. No Third-Party SDKs

TapTap Keys contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Local Storage Only

Song progress and purchase status are stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms (UserDefaults / StoreKit). This data never leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

TapTap Keys is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: support@taptapkeys.app
